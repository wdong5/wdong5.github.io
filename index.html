Wenqian Dong

Assistant Professor at Florida International University

Her research emphasizes on High-performance computing with a focus on (i) Scientific machine learning: accelerating HPC applications using machine learning-based approximation, (ii) Automatic Machine Learning: automatically machine learning model construction for HPC domain scientists, and (iii) Automatic Performance Tuning: performance optimization and quality control on scientific machine learning. Her work has been published in multiple top-tier conferences, including SC, ICS, EuroSys, VLDB. She has received her Ph.D. degree(Aug.2022) in EECS at University of California, Merced, advised by Prof. Dong Li. During the Ph.D., she did research at HP Labs and Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL). Her work at PNNL was highlighted at DOE and PNNL websites.
